Software

ALL software requirements should initially be directed to the Systems department.

For new software:

  • NO software development or new software package should be discussed with a supplier without Procurements and Systems knowledge or involvement.
  • NO commitment should be made to a supplier by any other department other than Procurement, this includes the negotiation of cost.
  • NO license or maintenance terms should be discussed/agreed with a supplier without Procurement or Systems involvement.

A PAR will be required for all orders of software.

Systems are responsible for the control and tracking of the licence certificates on behalf of the Bank.

It should be noted that the Bank is very strict regarding the installation of non-approved software licences. It is FORBIDDEN to install licences without the approval of Systems or Procurement.
